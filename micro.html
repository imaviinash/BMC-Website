About B.Sc. Microbiology:

Microbiology is a study of a minute living organism which cannot be seen with the naked eye. Recent trend of the industry focuses on production of totally new eukaryotic compounds like insulin, information and other recombination production using microorganism in large quantities. There are number of probiotic microbes being used in manufacturing of various food and pharmaceutical products, biofuels like bioethanol and biodesel are beings used in manufacturing of various food and pharmaceutical products. Many new recombinant vaccines are produced to replace crude vaccines and make them polyvalent to reduce the doses. Microbiology courses can be a good career option if you choose.

Eligibility : I.sc/+2 Science with Biology
Duration : 4 Years
Enrollment : 30 Students
