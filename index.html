INTRODUCTION

Arniko Foundation Nepal Pvt.Ltd is one of the well-known educational companies running different academic programs and founded by a group of highly experienced young educational profrssionals of Biratnagar. Birat Multiple College (BMC)is one of them which is established in 2069 for providing quality and need based education in a very peaceful and congenial academic environment. Our Courses

B.TECH. FOOD

Food Technology is a Branch of science in which the food science is applied in manufacturing and preserving of food products without loss of nutrients. The Food Technology studies the chemical, physical, and microbiological makeup of food. The food is processed, preserved, packaged and stored according to the speciications by industry and government. The reseaech and development in food technology has result into the production of safe and nutritious food. The food processing industries manufactue large varities of food products. They include the primary food like rice and wheat products, oil, sugar and pulses. They processed to convert into edible form. Food Technology courses can be a good career option.

B.Sc. CSIT

B.Sc. in Computer Science and Information Technology covers the theory and practise of designing and developing computer systems both hardware and software as well as networking and communication technologies. The program uses electronic devices, such as computer and mobile phones, computer software and network to store, process, transmit, retrieve and manipulate information. It has a lexible course structure, where students can choose to study a range of next generation technology areas including Digital media and games, mobile and computer application, enterprise informatics, energy informatics, computational mathematics, scientific computing and medical/Bio-informatics.

B.Sc. MICROBIOLOGY

Microbiology is a study of a minute living organism which cannot be seen with the naked eye. Recent trend of the industry focuses on production of totally new eukaryotic compounds like insulin, information and other recombination production using microorganism in large quantities. There are number of probiotic microbes being used in manufacturing of various food and pharmaceutical products, biofuels like bioethanol and biodesel are beings used in manufacturing of various food and pharmaceutical products. Many new recombinant vaccines are produced to replace crude vaccines and make them polyvalent to reduce the doses. Microbiology courses can be a good career option if you choose.
