About Us:

Birat Multiple College affiliated to Tribhuvan University is established in 2069 B.S. promoted by a group of experienced and dedicated academicians professionals. The goal of the college is to provide a high quality education that will enable the students to embrace the challenges of the modern world. The college focuses much on management and information technology in its academic voyage along with experimental and experiential learning. Apart from academic activities Birat Multiple college is associated with social program, blood donation program, quiz contestant and many more.

The college has organizational tie-ups with various software companies, IT consultancies, food manufacturing industries, factories and microbiologist industries in order to provide platform to conduct internship as well as option for job placement.

Academic Calendar:

The college strictly follows the academic calendar of T.U. Morever, it has its own calendar for the effective as well as smooth functioning of the classes and other programs. The schedule od class, practical, tests and exams and other co-curricular and extr-cirrcular are run accordance with our own schedule.


Admission Eligibility and Procedure:

Students who have passed +2 or equivalent must pass the entrance exam conducted by T.U. The successful students in entrance exam can apply on the college for the admission. The admission is done on first-come-first basis within the limited seats.


Uniform:

The college has prescribed some dress norms. All the students are strictly advised to follow the proper attire. Students must be in uniform whenever they enter into the college premises.
